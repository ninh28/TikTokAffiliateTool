## ✨ What Happens

Your original video gets transformed through 5 powerful steps:

```
Step 1: ✂️ TRIM
Remove 1s from start & end (clean edges)

Step 2: 🔍 ZOOM & CROP
1.1x zoom (removes original frame)

Step 3: ⚡ SPEED
1.1x speed (different timing)

Step 4: 🎨 VISUAL EFFECTS
Flip + Color grade + Grain overlay

Step 5: 🔊 AUDIO
Mix music + optional voiceover

RESULT: 1 Completely Different Video ✅
```
